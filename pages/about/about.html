ABOUT US

Located in the heart of the city, Green Bowl is a casual and vibrant eatery specializing in fresh, colorful salads and a variety of small, flavorful sides. Chef Noam Cohen, passionate about wholesome ingredients, crafts each dish with care, combining seasonal vegetables, house-made dressings, artisan breads, and complementary snacks like garlic bread and roasted nuts. Our philosophy is centered on freshness, simplicity, and a love for natural flavors. Guests are invited to enjoy a relaxed atmosphere where healthy eating meets delicious creativity, perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying a quick, satisfying meal... Our philosophy is rooted in sustainability, with ingredients sourced daily from local farms and markets. Every dish is carefully crafted, balancing traditional flavors with a modern twist. Guests are invited to enjoy an atmosphere that reflects warmth, community, and a deep respect for nature’s bounty.
